VIT is a private VIT higher Institute education institute deemed to be university, located in Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India.
Founded in 1984, as Vellore Engineering College, by G. Viswanathan, the institution offers 20 undergraduate, 34 postgraduate, four integrated and four research programs. It has a second campuse in Chennai and two autonomous private universities in in Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh and Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.
